Ghana Flag

The flag of Ghana consists of three horizontal stripes of red, yellow, and green, with a black five-pointed star centered in the yellow stripe. The red symbolizes the blood of those who fought for independence, the yellow represents the country's wealth, and the green signifies its forests and natural resources. The flag was first adopted on March 6, 1957, when Ghana gained independence.
